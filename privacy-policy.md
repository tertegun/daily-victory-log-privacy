# Privacy Policy for Daily Victory Log

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

Daily Victory Log is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Daily Victory Log does NOT collect any personal data.**

We do not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your browsing activity
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share information with third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Todo Tasks:** Your daily tasks and their completion status
- **Streak Information:** Your consecutive completion days
- **Tree Progress:** Visual representation of your progress
- **Completion History:** Record of completed tasks (stored locally only)

This data:
- Remains on your device at all times
- Is never transmitted over the internet
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions

The extension requires only one permission:

- **Storage Permission:** Used solely to save your todo list, streaks, and progress data locally on your device

This permission does not grant us access to your data. It only allows the extension to function properly by storing your tasks on your own device.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- We cannot access your data
- Your data never leaves your computer
- No internet connection is required
- No accounts or registration needed

## Third-Party Services

Daily Victory Log does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- External APIs

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Right-click the extension icon
2. Select "Remove from Chrome"
3. Confirm the removal

All stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

Alternatively, you can clear the extension's data:
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find "Daily Victory Log"
3. Click "Details"
4. Scroll down and click "Clear storage"

## Children's Privacy

Daily Victory Log does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.
